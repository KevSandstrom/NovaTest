Nova Maths Mentors


Nova Maths Mentors was founded by an accomplished tutor who believed in providing a better service to his students. It was observed that students will often feel confused in class due to the high pace of teaching or the uncomfortability of asking questions.

Our goal is to provide students with a comfortable learning environment where the pace of the lesson is adjusted for them to reach the best possible understanding of the Mathematics at hand. Nova's tutors also believe in the students abilities and motivate them to work harder, this builds up the students' confidence within Mathematics and allows them to start enjoying the subject.

Our tutors have no other commitments and can provide you with all year round attention, especially during exam time. Our vision is to assist every student with reaching their full Mathamatical capability and see Mathematics for the truly beautiful language it really is.
